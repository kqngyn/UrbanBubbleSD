About

Small shop.

Urban Bubble was born in San Diego with one conviction: boba could be more than a trend. More than sweetness and aesthetics.

We source our teas directly from growers, steep them at the right temperature, and build every drink from ingredients that actually mean something.

Real taro. Real matcha. Real milk. No artificial flavors. No shortcuts. Just tea made the way it deserves to be.
